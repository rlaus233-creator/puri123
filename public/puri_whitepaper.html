📊 PURI Index Exchange Concept

The PURI Hyper Index is a custom benchmark derived from global equity indicators such as the NASDAQ, transformed into a community-driven Web3 metric. Unlike traditional financial indices, the PURI Index is calculated on-chain through decentralized oracles and expressed in simplified units for easy understanding.

Within the PURIX Unified Exchange, the PURI Index powers prediction markets, gamified trading experiences, and experimental derivative-like structures — all while maintaining non-custodial, wallet-first settlement.

  • 🌐 Transparent oracle feeds with no hidden pricing
  • 🎮 Gamified “Yes/No” markets based on PURI Index milestones
  • 💱 Settlement supported in USDT, USDC, and $PURI
  • 🛡️ Structured as a community-driven experiment rather than a traditional financial product

This Index Exchange concept reinforces PURI’s identity as both a meme-born community currency and a serious experiment in decentralized market design.
